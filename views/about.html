Our Story

Markie Cadag Fashion was founded on a deep love of Filipino textile traditions and a belief that every person deserves to wear something beautiful, made just for them.

Who We Are

More Than a Fashion House

Founded by designer Markie Cadag, our atelier has grown from a local tailoring dream into a respected name in Filipino couture. We specialize in three pillars: bespoke custom pieces, limited-edition ready-to-wear, and heritage Barong & Filipiniana.

Every garment that leaves our studio carries our signature — a commitment to quality materials, expert construction, and designs that honor both tradition and modernity.

We are proud to be SEC, BIR, and Mayor's Permit registered — a testament to our commitment to running a legitimate, trustworthy business for our valued clients.
